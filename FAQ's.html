Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Dream Ticket

1. Dream Ticket kya hai?

Dream Ticket ek online lottery platform hai jisme aap WhatsApp ke madhyam se bhi lottery ticket purchase kar sakte hain aur daily draws me participate kar sakte hain.

2. WhatsApp se lottery kaise kharidein?

Aap WhatsApp par Dream Ticket ke official number se contact karein:
•“BUY” likhkar WhatsApp karein.
•Aapko available lotteries ki details bheji jayegi.
•Apni location aur desired lottery select karein.

3. Kya WhatsApp par ticket ka confirmation milega?

Haan, payment hone ke baad aapko WhatsApp par ticket number aur transaction ka confirmation message milega.

4. Lottery ka result kaise check karein?

•WhatsApp par “RESULT” likhkar bhejenge to aapko latest draw ka result mil jayega.
•Website ke "Results" section me bhi check kar sakte hain.

5. Agar jeet gaye to paise kaise milenge?

Agar aap winner hain, to aapko WhatsApp par notification milega aur aapka payment aapke UPI/bank account me transfer kar diya jayega.

6. WhatsApp payment options kaun kaun se hain?

Aap UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit card, ya net banking ke madhyam se payment kar sakte hain.

7. Kya WhatsApp lottery purchase safe hai?

Haan, agar aap kisi dost ko WhatsApp par refer karte hain aur wo ticket kharidta hai, to aapko special bonus ya free ticket mil sakti hai.,

8. Kya koi WhatsApp referral program hai?

Aap hamare WhatsApp, Email ya Toll-Free Number se customer support se contact kar sakte hain. Details website ke "Contact Us" section me available hain.
